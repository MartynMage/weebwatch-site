WatchWeeb is built with your privacy as the top priority. We collect the absolute minimum data necessary to provide you with a great anime tracking experience. Your watch list data stays on your device and in your personal iCloud account. We never sell your data or share it with third parties for advertising.

1. Introduction

Welcome to WatchWeeb ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the WatchWeeb mobile application (the "App").

WatchWeeb is an anime tracking application that allows you to:

  • Track anime you're watching, plan to watch, or have completed
  • Search and discover anime using the AniList database
  • Earn experience points (XP) and level up as you watch
  • Sync your watch list across your devices using iCloud

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Account Information

When you use Sign in with Apple:

  • Apple User ID: A unique, encrypted identifier provided by Apple. This is the ONLY piece of account information we store.
  • We do NOT collect: Your real name, email address, or any other personal information from Apple.

2.2 Optional Display Name

  • Display Name: You may optionally set a display name (nickname or handle) in your profile settings.
  • Privacy Notice: We explicitly warn users NOT to use their real name or email address as their display name. This is for display purposes only and may be visible if you enable social features in the future.

2.3 Watch List Data

We store information about the anime you track:

  • Anime titles and IDs (from AniList)
  • Your watch status (watching, completed, plan to watch, dropped)
  • Number of episodes you've watched
  • Your personal rating (if provided)
  • Notes you add to anime
  • Date added to your list

2.4 App Preferences

  • Theme preference (light, dark, or system)
  • Title display preference (English or Romaji)
  • Sort order preferences
  • Notification settings

2.5 Gamification Data

  • Total XP (experience points) earned
  • Current level
  • Statistics (total episodes watched, total time watched, etc.)

2.6 Cached Images

  • Anime cover images are cached locally on your device (up to 25 MB) to improve performance and enable offline viewing.
  • You can clear this cache at any time in Settings.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect solely to:

  • Provide the Service: Store and sync your watch list across your devices
  • Personalization: Display your statistics, XP, and level
  • Improve the App: Fix bugs and improve performance
  • Optional Features: If you enable social features in the future, your display name and public watch list may be visible to friends you connect with
We do NOT:
  • Use your data for advertising
  • Sell your data to third parties
  • Track your behavior for marketing purposes
  • Share your data with anyone except as described in this policy

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Local Storage

  • All of your watch list data is stored locally on your device using SwiftData (Apple's modern data persistence framework).
  • Your data remains on your device even when offline.

4.2 iCloud Sync

  • For Apple ID Users: Your watch list is automatically synced to your personal iCloud account and across all your devices signed in with the same Apple ID.
  • For Guest Users: Data is stored locally only on your device and does NOT sync to iCloud.
  • Important: iCloud data is stored in YOUR personal iCloud account, controlled by Apple. We do not have access to your iCloud data. Apple's iCloud Privacy Policy applies to data stored in iCloud.

4.3 Security Measures

  • We use industry-standard encryption for data transmission
  • Your Apple User ID is stored securely in iOS Keychain
  • All data is encrypted at rest on your device
  • iCloud data is encrypted in transit and at rest by Apple

5. Third-Party Services

5.1 AniList API

  • We use the AniList API to provide anime information, cover images, and search functionality.
  • When you search for anime, your search queries are sent to AniList's servers.
  • AniList's Privacy Policy applies to their service: https://anilist.co/terms
  • We do NOT share your watch list or personal information with AniList.

5.2 Sign in with Apple

  • We use Apple's Sign in with Apple service for authentication.
  • Apple's Privacy Policy applies: https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/
  • We receive only an encrypted User ID from Apple - no name or email.

5.3 Image Caching

  • We use the Kingfisher library to cache anime cover images locally on your device.
  • Images are downloaded from AniList's servers and stored locally for faster loading.
  • No tracking or analytics are performed by Kingfisher.

6. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do NOT sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for their marketing purposes.

We may disclose your information only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Legal Compliance: If required by law, regulation, legal process, or governmental request
  • Safety: To protect the safety, rights, or property of WatchWeeb, our users, or the public
  • Business Transfer: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets (users will be notified)
